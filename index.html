About

Born and raised in Indiana, my main passions were always across the board. My main hobbies growing up were Tae Kwon Do, photography, theatre, math, and engineering. This created a unique characteristic of being well rounded and learning to adapt to new situations.

Through college, I had always battled with deciding whether my analytical or creative side should win. What career will allow me to pull from both sides of my brain, and be something that I can wake up and enjoy every day? After much sole searching, and lots of angst, I found my love for web developement.

Since enrolling in Eleven Fifty Academy, I have embraced the coding lifestyle, and even found myself emmersed in a community of like-minded passionate people who want nothing but the best for others. I have found that passion can come in so many different shapes and sizes, and I am exstatic to start my career in a fast paced field.
